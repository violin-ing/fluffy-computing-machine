Wordle Solver

HOW TO USE

  • Step 1: The bot will give you a starting word.
  • Step 2: Type that word into the real (or imaginary) Wordle game.
  • Step 3: Click the tiles on this screen to match the colors you see in the real game (green, yellow, gray).
  • Step 4: Click SEND HINTS to get the next best guess.
  • Step 5: Repeat until the bot guesses the word (or you fucked something up and didn't send the right hint).
